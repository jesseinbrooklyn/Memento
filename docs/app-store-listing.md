## Description (4000 char max)

```
Most wellness apps talk to you like a child. Memento does not.

Memento is a daily Stoic practice built for men who want depth, not affirmation. No streaks. No badges. No push notifications begging you to come back. Just a serious tool for living with more clarity, purpose, and honesty.

REMEMBER YOU WILL DIE

Every morning begins with a confrontation. A personalized life calculator counts your remaining days based on your age and lifestyle. Not to frighten you — to wake you up. Marcus Aurelius opened every morning this way. So did Seneca. The most productive men in history started each day by remembering it could be their last.

SET YOUR INTENTION

After the reminder, you choose one intention for the day. Not a goal — an intention. "Speak honestly in the meeting." "Be present at dinner." Something concrete, tied to the man you are trying to become.

WISDOM WITH WEIGHT

A library of 100 quotes from the greatest Stoic thinkers — Marcus Aurelius, Seneca, Epictetus, Musonius Rufus — and adjacent voices like Montaigne, Viktor Frankl, and Lao Tzu. Every quote includes who said it, when they said it, and what they were facing at the time. These are not motivational posters. They are hard-won truths from men under real pressure.

YOUR LIFE IN DAYS

A visual life calculator renders your entire existence as a grid of days. Days lived. Days remaining. The ones you wasted and the ones you have left. Adjust for smoking, exercise, diet, and sleep to see how your choices shape the time you have.

REFLECT HONESTLY

Evening reflection drawn from Seneca's own practice. Three questions: What went well? Where did I fall short? What did not matter as much as I thought? No sugarcoating. No participation trophies. Just honest accounting.

WRITE WITHOUT AN AUDIENCE

A private journal with optional Stoic prompts. Freeform or guided. Everything stays on your device — no accounts, no cloud sync, no data collection. Your thoughts belong to you alone.

TRACK WITHOUT STREAKS

A practice calendar shows your consistency without gamifying it. Miss a day and nothing breaks. No guilt. No reset. Discipline is not a streak — it is a posture.

BUILT DIFFERENT

Memento has no backend. No analytics. No ads. No subscription. Your data never leaves your phone. The app works offline, loads instantly, and respects your attention. It is a tool, not a platform.

Dark Roman aesthetic. Cinzel and Cormorant Garamond typography. Gold on black. Built to feel like opening a leather-bound journal, not launching another app.

For the man who has read the Meditations and wants to live them.
```
*1,894 characters*
